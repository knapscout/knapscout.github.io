Step01

Open the Link

No app store. No download. It runs right in your phone's browser.

You got a link to KnapScout. Tap it and it opens in your phone's browser. That's the whole start, there's nothing to install from a store. KnapScout runs in the browser and lives on your home screen like any other app once you set it up, which we'll do at the end.

Step02

Sign In With Your Email

No password to create. No password to forget.

The first time in, the app asks for your email. Enter it and tap to continue. Instead of a password, KnapScout emails you a 6-digit code, so there's nothing to remember and nothing to reset later.

Step04

Set Your Name

It rides on every pin you drop. That's what keeps a group hunt straight.

The app asks for your name or initials. Whatever you enter gets stamped onto every pin you drop, so when you share a hunt with someone, they can tell your finds from theirs. Keep it short, a first name or initials is perfect.

Step05

Meet the Screen

A map, a status dot, and three buttons. That's the whole tool.

The map fills your screen, that's your ground. Across the top you'll see a status dot and your pin counts. The dot turns green when your GPS has a good fix.

Along the bottom are your three drop buttons, No Evidence, Evidence, and Find, each showing live GPS accuracy so you know your fix before you commit a pin.

That's all you need to recognize for now. How to actually survey with it, drop pins, draw a site, run a hunt, is covered in the Field Guide, linked at the bottom.

Step06

Put It on Your Home Screen

Full screen, one tap, no browser bar. Takes about ten seconds.

KnapScout works best as a tile on your home screen. The steps differ a little between iPhone and Android, so find yours below.

iPhone (Safari)

  1. Tap the Share button, the square with an arrow pointing up.
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add. It's now on your home screen like any app.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the menu, three dots in the top-right corner.
  2. Tap Add to Home screen (or Install app).
  3. Confirm. It's now on your home screen like any app.
Step07

Stay Signed In

Open the tile and go. No code, no re-login.

Once you're set up, you stay signed in on this device. Open the tile and you're ready, no code to re-enter, no logging back in. Your work is tied to your account.
